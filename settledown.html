Settle Down

By The 1975

"Settle Down" is a song by English rock band The 1975. It was released on 24 February 2014 as the sixth single from their self-titled debut studio album (2013). The song was written by band members George Daniel, Matthew Healy, Adam Hann and Ross MacDonald.
